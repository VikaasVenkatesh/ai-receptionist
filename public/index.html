How to Test the Demo

Steps

  1. 1

    Call the Twilio number

    Dial shown above in the idle panel from any phone. You'll hear a greeting.

  2. 2

    Watch this dashboard

    Keep this page open on a laptop. Every word you say and every AI reply appears here in real time.

  3. 3

    Ask about services or hours

    Try: "What services do you offer?" or "What are your hours?"

  4. 4

    Book an appointment

    Say: "I'd like to book an appointment." The AI will ask for your name, preferred time, and reason. Watch the green card appear here when it's confirmed.

Sample Scripts to Try

💬 General inquiry

"Hi, what kind of treatments do you offer for back pain?"

🕐 Hours question

"Are you open on weekends? What time do you close on Saturdays?"

📅 Book an appointment

"I hurt my lower back and I'd like to come in. My name is John Smith and I'm free this Thursday at 2pm."

🚗 Personal injury

"I was in a car accident last week and I have neck pain. Can I see Dr. Kim?"
